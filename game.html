Gyro Bowling

Click to launch the rotating cannon (square) ball.

The ball's path will be tangent to the circle it moves in.

The circular path's radius is changed based on the distance of the mouse from the center.

Press the space key to reverse the direction the ball rotates in.

After being launched, the ball will bounce along the borders of the window 3 times before stopping.

Try to knock out as many red targets as possible!
